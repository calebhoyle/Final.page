Stage 3

Redesign

In this stage of the project I had to figure out how to fix those flaws in the previous stage.
Firsly I made sure that the wings did not extrude through the center hole by adding a helix that
connected to the exterior of the hole instead of the center. After this I added a hole towards the top
of the propellor for the set screw. In the splicer I also made the wings wider and extend to
the top instead of 3/4 of the way, this was for more air resistance.
