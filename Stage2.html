First Print

After I finished my brainstorming process and I was happy with my design I was onto the first print.
I learned more about Onshape and how to use it, I then designed
my idea on the software. After I tried to use the 3d printers in class and discovered that my laptop could not run
the splicing software
required for the in class printers. After discovering this I emailed the file containing my
design to my groupmate Max, who agreed to print my project on his 3d printer at home.
When Max brought my print into class it had some flaws. The extrusions of the wings went through the hole in the middle making it impossible to spin on the shaft. The second flaw was that I forgot to
include a hole for the set screw that holds the propeller to the shaft.
